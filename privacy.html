Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Dark Verdict ("we", "us") respects your privacy. This policy explains how our content-publishing tools handle data.

1. Data We Access

Our integration accesses only the social media accounts that the channel owner explicitly authorizes (via official OAuth), and only to upload and manage the owner's own video content.

2. Data We Store

We store only the OAuth access tokens needed to publish content on the owner's behalf. Tokens are kept locally and never shared with third parties.

3. Data We Do NOT Collect

We do not collect, sell, or share any personal data of viewers or third parties. We do not access private messages or unrelated account data.

4. Third-Party Platforms

Use of TikTok, YouTube, Instagram and Facebook is governed by their respective privacy policies.

5. Data Deletion

Authorization can be revoked at any time from the platform's settings, which removes our access. To request deletion, contact us.
